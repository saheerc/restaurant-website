Our Story

Family recipes, since 1998

La Bella was founded by Chef Marco Rossi, who brought his grandmother's recipes from Naples to share with the world. Every dish is made fresh daily using imported Italian ingredients.

We believe dining is an experience — warm ambiance, attentive service, and food that makes you feel at home.
